xm. 21. dotium is an emendation by Pros. A. Palmer. The MSS.
have notaque et artium. Prof. Housman proposed nota quot artium,
but it is difficult to see what artes a lady’s sace could have. On the
other hand dotes (gists, endowments) are frequently ascribed to a sace or
figure. (See Lewis and Short, s.v. dos.)
